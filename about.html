Our Company

Imperium Opes is a leading provider of integrated engineering services and solutions for the supply chain industry. Our offerings include the provision of industrial electrical and mechanical products, as well as logistics services.

Headquartered in Dar Es Salaam, Tanzania, we strive to be a market leader in the provision of industrial products and solutions that exceed customer expectations, both locally and internationally. At Imperium Opes, we are dedicated to delivering exceptional products and services and are committed to ongoing improvement in order to better serve our valued clients.

OUR MISSION

To create and deliver competitive value to our customers and sustainable returns to our shareholders.

OUR VISION

To create and deliver competitive value to our customers and sustainable returns to our shareholders.

OUR VALUES

Our core values are driven by the desire to provide premium and uncompromising solutions to our customers.


This is what inspires our mission to become the supreme knowledge source and solution provider for integrated supply services in electrical and mechanical products and services, industrial automation supplier and logistics services.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from the products and services we offer to the level of customer service we provide. We are committed to delivering the highest quality solutions that exceed customer expectations and set us apart as the industry leader in our field

Knowledge Dominance
